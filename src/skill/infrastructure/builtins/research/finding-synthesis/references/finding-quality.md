# Finding Quality

Every finding needs evidence. Do not promote a note into a finding unless it cites source IDs, workspace paths, or both.

Required fields, in order: Claim, Scope, Confidence, Verified at, Evidence, Notes.

Confidence values:
- high: direct, current evidence agrees across reliable sources.
- medium: evidence is useful but partial, older, or from fewer sources.
- low: evidence is indirect, disputed, incomplete, or needs rechecking.

Use Notes for caveats, contradictions, and next checks.
