# Finding Quality

Every finding needs evidence. Write a claim only when it can point to source IDs, workspace paths, or both.

Use fields this way:
- Claim: one testable statement.
- Scope: where the claim applies and where it may not.
- Confidence: `high`, `medium`, or `low` based on source agreement, freshness, and directness.
- Verified at: the ISO date when the evidence was checked.
- Evidence: source IDs such as `W001`, `D001`, or `F001`.
- Notes: caveats, contradictions, and next checks.

Prefer lower confidence when evidence is indirect, stale, incomplete, or disputed.
